Frequently Asked Questions

What is Moleculogic?

Moleculogic is a tiered simulation and design platform for programmable therapeutics. It allows controlled, logic-based modeling of molecular behavior across charge, pH, redox, and coordination systems.

Is Moleculogic an AI platform?

No. Moleculogic is not based on black-box generative AI. It uses logic-structured inputs, validation overlays, and defined behavioral conditionsâ€”not probabilistic molecule guessing or data scraping.

Do I own what I create?

Yes. You own all validated therapeutic outputs generated within Tier 2 or higher, governed under our simulation access and milestone agreement. MetalDrug retains ownership of the core logic system and verification overlays.

Can I integrate Moleculogic into my lab?

Yes, under Tier 3 or white-label deployment agreements. This allows secure local simulation using Moleculogic's sandbox engine while maintaining compliance and auditability.

How is Moleculogic different from AI drug discovery?

AI drug platforms often rely on pattern recognition from past compounds. Moleculogic designs new behavior using logic-first simulations that are independently verifiable and structurally programmable.
