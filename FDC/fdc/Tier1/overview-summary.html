Functional Drug Complex (FDC) - Tier 1 Overview

This document outlines the foundational principles, architecture, and differentiators of the FDC platform as made accessible to Tier 1 reviewers.

Key Concepts

  • Beyond Metal Complexes: FDC incorporates proteins, peptides, functional ligands, and adaptive small molecules.
  • Tiered Complexity: FDC compounds are classified using entropy scoring, therapeutic targeting, and synthetic stability factors.
  • Integration-Ready: Built to interoperate with Moleculogic, SEI-Lite scoring, and SEI-Core simulation layers.

What You Can Access at Tier 1

  • Summary dossiers on current functional classes under study
  • Insights into the design framework used for FDC molecules
  • Example pseudocode and SEI-Lite simulation outputs
