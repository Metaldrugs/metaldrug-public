What is FDC?

The Functional Drug Complex (FDC) platform extends Moleculogic by enabling simulation and validation of non-metal drug interactions, protein behavior, and compound assembly. FDC unlocks next-gen drug design for diverse therapeutic applications.

Platform Capabilities

  • Functional Compound Simulation
  • Protein-Complex Analysis
  • Context-Driven Efficacy Evaluation
  • Pathway Integration with Moleculogic Tier Systems
