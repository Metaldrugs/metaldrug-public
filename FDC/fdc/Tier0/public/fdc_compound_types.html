Functional Drug Complex Types

Supported and prioritized molecular classes in Moleculogic-FDC simulations.

Supported Compound Categories

  • Small Molecule Complexes – Standard therapeutics enhanced with logical or metal coordination rules.
  • Metal-Organic Hybrids – Includes ion-coordinated small molecules or organic cages with functional cores.
  • Peptide-Linked Assemblies – Short peptide sequences integrated with logic-based chemical groups.
  • AI-Predictive Synthetic Nodes – Molecules generated via SEI-lucidcore logic path exploration.
  • Bioelectronic Bridge Compounds – Functional groups designed for signal modulation and control.

Excluded or Under Review

  • Unstable radicals with high reactivity in simulation space
  • Complex biologics beyond simulation limits
  • CAR-T analogs (not currently supported in Moleculogic logic tree)
