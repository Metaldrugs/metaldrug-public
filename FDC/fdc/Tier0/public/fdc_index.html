Welcome to FDC Public Tier 0

The Functional Drug Complex (FDC) platform introduces a modular path to post-AlphaFold therapeutic development. Unlike traditional discovery tools, FDC combines functional compound logic, SEI-lite overlay scoring, and tiered access for scalable evaluation.

This public page grants open access to preview documents, concept overviews, ethical declarations, and submission portals for deeper access tiers.
