FDC Platform - Use Case Briefs

This document provides illustrative Tier 1-level briefs showcasing the functional advantages and applications of the FDC platform.

1. Functional Nanocarriers for CNS Disorders

Design of peptide-coordinated delivery complexes capable of traversing the blood-brain barrier with high entropy tolerance and metabolic survivability.

2. Multi-Target Autoimmune Regulation

Construction of FDC compounds that dynamically adjust immunosuppressive payload release in response to local ROS concentration and pH gradients.

3. Tunable Anti-Cancer Scaffolds

FDCs synthesized to morphologically adapt between solid tumor and leukemia environments, modifying target affinities via SEI-Lite feedback.

4. Vaccine Amplification Additives

Integration of FDCs as adjuvant enhancers that boost T-cell activation in mRNA and protein vaccine platforms with minimal toxicity.

Access to mechanistic simulations, formulation parameters, and SEI logic for each use case is available under Tier 2 access.
