Ethics and Access

Commitment to Ethical Innovation

The Functional Drug Complex (FDC) platform is committed to ethical discovery, transparency, and equitable access across global health systems. This includes the intentional exclusion of exploitative licensing and AI-generated compounds with dual-use concerns.

Access Framework

  • Tier 0: Public preview only - no experimental access.
  • Tier 1: Requires NDA and basic review agreement.
  • Tier 2: Reserved for vetted collaborators with published intent.
  • Tier 3: Highest-level access - strictly governed by licensing board.

Review and Licensing Committee

A multidisciplinary ethics board will oversee access requests and licensing eligibility. This includes scientific advisors, legal experts, bioethics professionals, and KT governance.

AI Oversight

All AI-generated compounds are audited using the Moleculogic-SEI stack. No logic or molecule may proceed to Tier 3 deployment without clearance from SEI reflexive simulation and ethical filters.
