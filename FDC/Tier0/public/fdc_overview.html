Functional Drug Complex (FDC) Overview

Back to Index | Tier Access | Use Cases

The FDC framework represents the next logical progression after AlphaFold, transitioning from *predictive structure modeling* to *functional therapeutic design*.

While AlphaFold revolutionized how we understand protein folding, FDC addresses:

  • How functional complexes behave in physiological conditions
  • Cross-domain compounds beyond proteins - including peptides, ligands, non-covalent assemblies, metal-integrated agents, and more
  • Ethical access pathways, usage scoring, and systematized licensing for global deployment

FDC is not bound to a single AI model but is modularly interpreted by the SEI ecosystem, including SEI-lite and SEI-core with LucidCore simulation.

It supports research institutions, pharma companies, and global health agencies by offering a tiered framework to explore, validate, and adopt novel therapeutics.
