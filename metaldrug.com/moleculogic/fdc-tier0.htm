Functional Drug Complex (FDC) Tier 0 Preview

This page introduces our new class of programmable non-metal therapeutics: the Functional Drug Complex (FDC). Tier 0 provides a non-confidential public look into how FDCs simulate, score, and validate novel drug frameworks.

FDC Capabilities

  • Simulates ligands, peptides, RNAs, and hybrid biologic-drug frameworks
  • Visual scoring overlays based on entropy, activation energy, and ROS potential
  • Interactive molecule inspector for tier-based submission
