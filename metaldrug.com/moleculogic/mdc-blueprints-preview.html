Metal-Drug Complex Platform

The Metal-Drug Complex (MDC) platform represents a next-generation leap in pharmaceutical design that leverages coordination chemistry to create programmable therapeutics with intelligent behavior.

Logic-Based Activation

MDCs activate only under specific conditions (pH, ROS, enzymes) for precise drug delivery.

Modular Architecture

Metal cores and ligands can be interchanged like components of a logic circuit.

SEI-Lite Integration

AI-powered inference optimizes ligand selection and predicts MDC behavior.

Blueprint System

Every MDC is defined by a blueprint with traceable IP and version history.
