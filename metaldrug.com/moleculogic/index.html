Moleculogic: Structured Therapeutic Intelligence

Moleculogic is the logic simulation environment behind all MetalDrug design flows. Built for traceability, tiered access, and compound programming, it is a structured system for exploring therapeutic logic—not a black-box AI or a molecule randomizer.

Interactive Logic Architecture

Each molecule inside Moleculogic is built on logic scaffolds: conditions, triggers, simulations, and risk overlays. Moleculogic supports electrostatic, redox, peptide, ligand, and RNA-interaction modeling through its modular compound interface.

Tiered System Access

  • Tier 0: Public overview of molecule logic structure types (no simulation access).
  • Tier 1: NDA-gated logic viewer and blueprint previewer, with basic entropy overlays.
  • Tier 2: Secure compound designer, with programmable states, input parameters, and verification gates.
  • Tier 3+: Full traceable sandbox with co-development, logic exports, and lifecycle monitoring tools.

Blueprint-Driven Therapeutics

Every candidate starts as a blueprint—a logical representation of a compound’s potential interactions and conditions. Moleculogic stress-tests blueprints in simulated biological environments before they enter any lab.

Two Tracks, One Engine

  • MDC (Metal Drug Complexes): Metal-coordinated compounds guided by energy surfaces, charge-based interaction maps, and multi-ligand binding logic.
  • FDC (Functional Drug Complexes): Peptide, protein, RNA, and synthetic organic logic systems modeled for programmable therapeutics.

Logic Is the New Molecule

We don’t generate drug candidates—we generate logical constructs that can become drugs. Every blueprint has a trail. Every trail has validation gates. Moleculogic turns compound exploration into an engineered process.
