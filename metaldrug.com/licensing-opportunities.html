Licensing Opportunities

MetalDrug introduces a new licensing model that reflects the programmable nature of modern therapeutic platforms. Rather than static compound ownership, we offer collaborative tiers that align with simulation access, molecule evolution stages, and ethical oversight.

1. Tiered Simulation-Based Licensing

Moleculogic operates on a tiered logic access structure. Each tier corresponds to the maturity and controllability of the molecular blueprint:

  • Tier 0 - Public simulation preview with basic interaction models (view-only).
  • Tier 1 - NDA-gated access to simulation outputs, logic overlays, and entropy/risk previews.
  • Tier 2 - Partnership-tier for guided simulation evolution and regulatory-aligned therapeutic programming.
  • Tier 3+ - Embedded co-development of novel compounds under milestone and IP-gated co-ownership.

2. Flexible Licensing Structures

Whether you're a pharmaceutical company, university, synthetic biology group, or biotech incubator, we offer access frameworks designed to align with your needs:

  • Logic-Linked Molecule Licenses - Licenses are granted not just for molecules, but for the validated logic layers that define them.
  • MDC/FDC-as-a-Service - We offer runtime access to Moleculogic’s simulation infrastructure via secure, NDA-backed APIs.
  • Therapeutic Blueprint Leasing - Ideal for research institutions and innovation hubs seeking short-term access to logic-evolved candidates.
  • White-Label Molecular Design Systems - Deploy Moleculogic simulation logic internally under your brand, while maintaining our logic verification layer.

3. IP Ownership & Simulation Traceability

Every simulation blueprint is hashed, time-stamped, and traceable. Partners retain commercial rights to derivatives, while core logic layers remain governed under joint simulation audits and ethical simulation protocols.

We protect your IP without compromising our simulation integrity. Moleculogic operates as an ethics-embedded simulation platform—no uncontrolled molecule generation, no opaque AI shortcuts.
